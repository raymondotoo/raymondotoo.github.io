Case Study — Multi-Omics Integration

Aligning transcriptome and proteome signals with mixOmics

Integrated matched RNA-seq and TMT proteomics to pinpoint convergent pathways driving irradiation-induced cognitive deficits and SFN responses.

mixOmics Correlation circle Circos concordance FGSEA / IPA overlays

Objective

Integrate transcript and protein readouts to surface convergent pathways and therapeutic targets in irradiation + SFN models.

Approach

  • Pre-filtered genes/proteins for reliability, scaled/centered for cross-omics correlation.
  • mixOmics correlation circle to visualize mRNA-protein concordance; Circos plot to link paired features.
  • Overlaid FGSEA/IPA pathway summaries to prioritize actionable targets.

Key Findings

  • Neuroinflammation, mitochondrial dysfunction, and synaptic disruption were the dominant convergent signals.
  • Highlighted candidates such as CREB and PSEN1 for follow-up validation.
  • Noted limits: few DE proteins and modest mRNA-protein correlations, informing future sample sizing.
