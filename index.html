About Me

I'm a Bioinformatics Scientist with 4+ years of experience at the intersection of bioinformatics research and machine learning. I specialize in multi-omics data analysis and in building reproducible workflows that uncover insights from large-scale sequencing data, with the goal of driving biomarker discovery and advancing disease modeling. My current work focuses on uncovering risk factors and molecular mechanisms underlying Alzheimer’s disease and traumatic brain injury through integrative analysis of terabyte-scale proteomics, transcriptomics, and neuroimaging data. Outside of research, I enjoy traveling and photography, passions that inspire my curiosity and creativity.
