Case Study — Transcriptomics

Cranial irradiation +/- sulforaphane (SFN) in juvenile hippocampus

Bulk RNA-seq (nf-core/rnaseq) on hippocampal tissue to reveal how 10 Gy irradiation impacts cognition-linked pathways and whether SFN mitigates those effects.

nf-core/rnaseq PCA (top 500 genes) Volcano + top-40 heatmap FGSEA

Objective

Determine how cranial irradiation alters hippocampal gene expression and whether SFN modulates those signatures.

Workflow

Pipeline nf-core/rnaseq
QC PCA on top 500 variable genes
DE Volcano plot + ranked heatmap (top 40)
Focus Mitochondria, synapse, inflammation

PCA confirmed clean separation of treatment groups; DE genes were prioritized and visualized with volcano and heatmap views for quick hypothesis generation.

Key Results

  • Highlighted genes: Atp6v1h, Fer1l5, Hibch (mitochondrial/metabolic); Nfkbie upregulated; TTN and SLC39A6 downregulated.
  • Pathways disrupted: neuronal signaling, synaptic function, and inflammatory cascades.
  • Findings dovetail with proteomics and microbiome results for a cohesive mechanistic story.
