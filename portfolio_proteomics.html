Case Study — Proteomics (TMT-MS)

Hippocampal proteome shifts after cranial irradiation

Quantified TMT-10plex hippocampal proteomes to pinpoint irradiation-driven changes and assess sulforaphane (SFN) modulation. Built a DEP pipeline (VSN + MinProb) and pathway modeling to prioritize therapeutic targets.

TMT-10plex Orbitrap Eclipse MaxQuant -> DEP IPA / FGSEA

Objective

Quantify hippocampal protein changes after 10 Gy irradiation and evaluate whether SFN modulates long-term molecular damage.

Workflow

Acquisition TMT-10plex, Orbitrap Eclipse
Processing MaxQuant -> DEP (VSN + MinProb)
Significance |log2FC| ≥ 1.5 & adj. p ≤ 0.05
Interpretation FGSEA + IPA networks

DEP thresholds prioritized high-confidence proteins; IPA and FGSEA contextualized pathways and upstream regulators relevant to neuronal signaling and inflammation.

Key Results

  • PCA cleanly separated treatment groups, indicating irradiation-driven proteome shifts.
  • Differential proteins mapped to neuronal signaling, synaptic function, and inflammatory pathways.
  • Candidate nodes emerging from IPA/FGSEA inform follow-up validation and therapeutic targeting.
